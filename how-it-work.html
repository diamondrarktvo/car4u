How it work

CAR4U in three steps

Step 1. Online Advert

The owner create an online advert with photos and a description of the vehicle. He also set his own price and configure the availability schedule for his car.

Step 2. Request

After checking his profile, the borrower can send a request for the cars that match his needs. If the request is accepted, Payment is made directly online.

Step 3. Booking

The borrower and the owner meet at the beginning of the booking to make the inventory of fixtures and the exchange of keys. The borrower can drive safely, His journey is insured through our partner and we offer him a customer service available at any time.
